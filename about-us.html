About Bharatgas

Our Legacy

With over 40 years of excellence, Bharatgas, the flagship brand of Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL), has become a trusted name in the LPG sector. Serving more than 9.35 crore customers nationwide, our motto, 'Cook Food Serve Love', reflects our commitment to providing safe, reliable, and clean cooking fuel to millions of Indian households.

Our Offerings

Domestic LPG: Bharatgas cylinders for households come in various sizes to suit every family's needs.

Commercial LPG: Bulk LPG solutions for restaurants, hotels, and businesses ensure efficiency and safety.

Industrial Solutions: Customized energy solutions for manufacturing, processing, and other industrial applications.

Our Commitment

Safety is at the heart of Bharatgas operations. With stringent safety standards, regular training programs, and state-of-the-art technology, we ensure the highest level of care in LPG production and delivery.

Bharatgas also supports sustainability by promoting LPG as a cleaner alternative to traditional fuels, playing a vital role in reducing carbon emissions and advancing environmental goals in India.

Customer-Centric Approach

With a robust distribution network, seamless online booking options, and responsive customer service, Bharatgas ensures a hassle-free experience for all its customers. Our mobile app and Urja Chatbot bring convenience to your fingertips.
